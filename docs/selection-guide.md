# 选型指南 / Selection Guide

## 按需求选择

| 需求 | 首选 | 次选 | 不建议一开始使用 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 中文论文润色与Prompt | awesome-ai-research-writing | ChatGPT-Academic-Prompt | 完整自动科研系统 |
| Codex逐章节修改 | academic-research-skills-codex | Research-Paper-Writing-Skills | 一次加载上百个Skills |
| 论文最终QC | academic-writing-agents | paper-writing-skill | 只使用单一润色Prompt |
| AI实验与论文联动 | AI-Research-SKILLs | scientific-agent-skills | 无隔离环境的自动执行 |
| 自动科研研究 | AutoResearchClaw | AI-Scientist / v2 | 把生成论文直接投稿 |
| 中文科研方法训练 | learning_research | 本仓库工作流 | 仅追求“降AI率” |

## 决策原则

1. 先区分“写作辅助”“实验工程”“自动科研”三种需求。
2. 每个项目只选择一个主编排工作流。
3. 章节写作、引用核验、最终审稿应拆成不同质量门禁。
4. 对可执行代码的Skill进行权限、安全和依赖审查。
5. 任何自动生成引用都必须回到出版商页面、DOI或论文原文验证。

## By use case

| Need | Primary choice | Secondary choice | Avoid initially |
|---|---|---|---|
| Chinese polishing and prompts | awesome-ai-research-writing | ChatGPT-Academic-Prompt | Full autonomous-research systems |
| Section-by-section Codex editing | academic-research-skills-codex | Research-Paper-Writing-Skills | Loading hundreds of skills at once |
| Final paper QC | academic-writing-agents | paper-writing-skill | A single polishing prompt only |
| AI experiments linked to writing | AI-Research-SKILLs | scientific-agent-skills | Unsandboxed autonomous execution |
| Autonomous-research study | AutoResearchClaw | AI-Scientist / v2 | Direct submission of generated papers |
| Research methodology training | learning_research | This hub's workflow | Optimizing only for detector scores |
