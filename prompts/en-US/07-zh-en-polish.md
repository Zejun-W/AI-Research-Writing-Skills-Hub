# Chinese-to-English Academic Writing Prompt

Stage 1: Repair logic, ambiguity, terminology, and redundancy in Chinese without changing technical facts.
Stage 2: Translate into accurate natural academic English while preserving equations, citations, variables, and numbering.
Stage 3: Polish for computer-science publication style, avoiding literal translation, generic templates, excessive nominalization, and exaggerated claims.

Return the final English, a bilingual terminology list, and ambiguous sentences that require author decisions.
